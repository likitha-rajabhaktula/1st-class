Sweets are popular confections enjoyed globally for their sweet taste and are often associated with celebrations, comfort, and religious ceremonies. They come in a vast variety of forms, from baked and fried goods to frozen desserts, and are made from a wide range of ingredients including sugar, milk, fruits, nuts, and different types of flour. Sweets play a significant role in many cultures, serving as a symbol of happiness, and are often exchanged during festivals, weddings, and other special occasions.

Bakery items are a diverse category of food products made from flour and baked in an oven, including staples like bread and rolls, as well as sweets such as cakes, cookies, pastries, and pies. These products vary widely in texture, flavor, and ingredients, and can be classified by their sugar content, leavening agents, or moisture levels. From everyday sustenance to celebratory treats, bakery items are a significant part of many cuisines and diets worldwide.
